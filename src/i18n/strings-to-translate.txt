story
Born in Ho Chi Minh City. Landed in Minnesota on Valentine's Day 1998, age four, with my parents and sister. Grew up in Eagan — studied computer science and IT infrastructure at the University of Minnesota.
Before tech I did other work. A summer stamping metal at my dad's shop. A summer pulling crab cages with my uncle in Louisiana bayous — 3am to 3pm, Gulf of Mexico heat. Years pulling espresso at Caribou Coffee and bartending through school. That work shaped how I think about craft: slow is fast, and the person next to you matters more than the tool in your hand.
Tech career started at Target in 2017. Then Zoom, Google, and now SmartNews in Tokyo — where I'm a founding engineer on NewsArc. I'm near Shibuya, learning Mandarin, resuming Korean, and trying to move to Kichijoji by the end of 2026.
